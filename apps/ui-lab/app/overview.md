# Overview

`apps/ui-lab/app`

## Purpose

UI catalogue routes and persistent Lab shell. Everything here is fixture-only and must not call product APIs or become a
second source of product behavior.

## Subfolders

- `components/` — shared primitive inventory and state examples.
- `compositions/` — disposable information-hierarchy studies.
- `flows/` — scenario and exceptional-state simulators.
- `patterns/` — reusable layout and interaction pattern demonstrations.
- `styles/` — Lab-only workbench styling.

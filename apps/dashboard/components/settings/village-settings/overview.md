# Overview

`apps/dashboard/components/settings/village-settings`

## Purpose

Village settings master/detail feature. The tab owns pane composition, while the list and editor own their respective
selection and form presentation.

## Key files

- `village-settings-tab.tsx` — master/detail composition and responsive backdrop.
- `village-list-pane.tsx` — searchable village selection pane.
- `village-editor-pane.tsx` — selected-village form and actions.
